About

Hi there! As you know, my name is Sandhya. I love helping people gain access to quality resources and connecting with community. I first did it as an analyst, helping refugee serving organizations, organizations that serve youth experiencing homelessness, and health promoting organizations provide the best quality of service to the greatest number of people they could. Later, I helped people reach for their dreams of working in an English-speaking environment and connecting to people from different cultures as an English teacher.

Interestingly enough, it was through conversations with some of my students that I started thinking about web development. You see, throughout my professional experience, my favorite projects involved creating tangible tools. I had been thinking about how to expand on that when conversations about web development came up with my students. I realized it was the perfect skill to support my efforts in creating quality, accessible tools. So, I took a front-end development course with SheCodes to learn the basics and started bringing my ideas to life. As they say, the rest is history!
